==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on November 27, 2021 at 6:42 PM GMT

It includes 2103 images.
Walmart-product are annotated in clip format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 480x480 (Fit within)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
