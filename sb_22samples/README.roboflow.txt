
San Bruno Lab full resolution data - v5 Jae_test_1920x1080_clip
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on November 27, 2021 at 5:10 AM GMT

It includes 2103 images.
Walmart-product are annotated in clip format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 1920x1080 (Fill (with center crop))

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
